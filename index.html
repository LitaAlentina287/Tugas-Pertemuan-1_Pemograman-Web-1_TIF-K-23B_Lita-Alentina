The Best Movies According to Lita Alentina

My top 3 movies of all-time.

1. On The Spot

Type: Entertainment & information program.

Content: Presents a collection of unique, strange, mysterious, and interesting facts from around the world. For example: “7 Strangest Animals in the World”, “Mystery of the Lost City”, “Unique Natural Phenomena”.

Purpose: To entertain while adding knowledge through a light and engaging presentation style.

2. Jejak Si Gundul

Type: Social documentary.

Content: Tells the journey of a bald man (Gundul) who visits various remote places across Indonesia. He highlights the lives of simple people, inspiring stories, struggles, and local wisdom.

Purpose: To inspire viewers through real stories of hard work and perseverance from ordinary people around us.

3. Laptop Si Unyil

Type: Children & family educational program.

Content: Features the puppet character Si Unyil and his friends, discussing technology, knowledge, and scientific discoveries in a way that is easy for children to understand. For example: robots, modern transportation, or how simple machines work.

Purpose: To provide education in a fun way so that children can learn while watching.
